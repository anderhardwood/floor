Services

Anderson Hardwood Floors offers various amounts of services to its customers.
and performs them all with love and care

1.Installation
2.Finish
3.Refinishing
4.Spindles
5.Posts

INSTALLATION

Installation is the process of installing the hardwood floor to a
subfloor with either nails, glue, or staples.

FINISH

A finish is a top coat of water-based or oil-based stain, that gives the floor its nice
every day color. Finish is an excellent method to personilze your hardwood floor.

REFINISH

Refinishing is a process of removing the top layer of the wood, so that the raw and fresh wood
underneath may be revealed, so that the process of finishing may commence following this process.

SPINDLES

We install spindles on your stairs, etc. It is a simple process of connecting the base rail and hand rail
on a staircase, etc.The handrail goes on top on the spindles once they are installed.

POSTS

We also install posts, cutting them to the desired length before securing it to the floor
with lag bolt, mointing plate, etc.
